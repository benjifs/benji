For such a massive ensemble cast, every single performance was very memorable.

I was initially thrown off by the [needle drops](https://en.wiktionary.org/wiki/needle_drop#:~:text=2.) more so because they felt out of time but they actually really work.

<spoiler>I bounced around with the rating for this mostly because I was left a little conflicted by the ending. I think its purposely ambiguous to make us think about whether Marty has actually grown as a person. Specially if I may not necessarily feel like he deserves to win (both in the game and life) based on everything we saw the prior two hours. Probably my favorite vampire film of the year, so far.</spoiler>
